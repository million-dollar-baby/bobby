Delhi 47 Km

Delhi 47 Km is a Hindi movie released on 20 Jul, 2018. The movie is directed by Shadab Khann and featured Bobby Vats, Dolly Tomar, Shadab Khann, Rajneesh Dubey and Neetiy Yadav as lead characters.

Bluffmaster

a 2005 Bollywood romance comedy film directed by Rohan Sippy and produced by Ramesh Sippy. The film stars Abhishek Bacchan, Ritesh Deshmukh and Priyanka Chopra in the lead roles, along with Boman Irani, Nana Patekar and Bobby Vats in supporting roles.

Aa Dekhein Zaraa

Aa Dekhen Zara is a 2009 romantic sci-fi action thriller film starring Neil Nitin Mukesh, who plays a photo journalist, and Bipasha Basu, as a disc jockey. The film is directorial debut of Jehangir Surti. Bobby Vats played the role of a RAW agent as the primary antagonist in this film.

James

James is 2005 Bollywood film starring Mohit Ahlawat and Nisha Kothari. It was directed by Rohit Jugraj.Bobby Vats played an important role in this film.

KLPD

Invincible Entertainment presents Kismet Love Paisa Dilli is a 2012 Bollywood comedy thriller film directed by Sanjay Khanduri. The film features Vivek Oberoi opposite Malika Sherawat in lead roles. Bobby Vats played the role of a gay fashion designer in this film with shades of grey.
